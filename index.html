About

I am a Ph.D. student in Politics with subfields in International Relations and Research Methods. My research focuses on security studies, military signaling, cognitive warfare, and technology policy in the Indo-Pacific.

I serve as the Associate Director of a major open-source intelligence project, regularly briefing senior defense and intelligence officials. My work spans academic publications, policy commentary, wargame design, and AI-driven early warning systems.
